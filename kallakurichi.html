History and Geography

Kallakurichi, a district in Tamil Nadu, was officially formed on November 26, 2019, after being separated from Villupuram district. The region has a rich historical background, having been influenced by the Chola, Pandya, and Vijayanagara empires. During the 18th century, it was under the rule of the Arcot Nawabs before coming under British control. Known for its agricultural significance, the district thrives on crops like paddy, sugarcane, and tapioca. In recent years, Kallakurichi has been emerging as an educational and industrial hub, contributing to the state's overall development.
